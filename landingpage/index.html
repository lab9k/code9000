Meet Bert

Bert is a bird lover and photographer. Currently he likes taking pictures at the Houtdok in Ghent. Recently people have been spotting the common tern. A bird that looks like the logo at the top. Bert is very intrested in this little bird, but he doesn’t want too miss a chance of missing any other birds. So he takes pictures of all the birds he sees. Can you help him sort his pictures?
